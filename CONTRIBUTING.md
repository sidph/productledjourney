# Contributing to Product Led Journey

Thanks for your interest in contributing.

Product Led Journey is a collection of lessons, ideas, and stories from building products, platforms, and teams. The goal is simple: share practical insights that help builders learn faster.

## Ways to Contribute

Contributions can be big or small:

* Fix typos, formatting, or broken links
* Improve existing articles
* Suggest new topics
* Share books, podcasts, or resources
* Contribute guest posts or case studies
* Add examples, visuals, or real-world experiences

If you're unsure whether something is a good fit, feel free to open an issue first.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a branch for your changes
3. Submit a Pull Request
4. I'll review it and provide feedback if needed
5. Once approved, it will be published

All contributions are reviewed before merging.

## What Makes a Great Contribution

* Practical over theoretical
* Clear and concise
* Real-world examples
* Respectful and constructive
* Focused on helping builders learn

Topics can include product management, developer experience, platform strategy, AI, fintech, startups, security, career lessons, and learning resources.

## Final Thought

The best lessons often come from launches, mistakes, experiments, customer conversations, and hard-earned experience. If you have something valuable to share, I'd love to hear from you.

Happy building 🚀
