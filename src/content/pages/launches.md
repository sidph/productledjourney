I’ve always thought of my career less like a résumé and more like a series of product launches - each one a season finale with its own cliffhangers, plot twists, and “how did we ship that?” moments.

Below are a few of my favorite builds across very different industries. Each company, a couple of my favorite launches, and the ripple effects that followed.
